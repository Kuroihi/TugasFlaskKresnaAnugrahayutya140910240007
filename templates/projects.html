Water Level Monitoring System

This project was developed as the final assignment for the Pemrograman I course. It leverages the portability and versatility of the Arduino Nano, along with the affordability of the HC-SR04 ultrasonic proximity sensor. Data collected by the sensor is processed using an Arduino Uno and visualized on a 0.96-inch OLED display.
